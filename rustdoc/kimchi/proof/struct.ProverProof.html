Expand description

The proof that the prover creates from a [ProverIndex] and a witness.

Fields

commitments: ProverCommitments<G>

All the polynomial commitments required in the proof

proof: OpeningProof<G>

batched commitment opening proof

evals: [ProofEvaluations<Vec<ScalarField<G>>>; 2]

Two evaluations over a number of committed polynomials

ft_eval1: ScalarField<G>

Required evaluation for Maller’s optimization

public: Vec<ScalarField<G>>

The public input

prev_challenges: Vec<(Vec<ScalarField<G>>, PolyComm<G>)>

The challenges underlying the optional polynomials folded into the proof
